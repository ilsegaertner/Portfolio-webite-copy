About Me

Hey, I'm Joachim, a web developer driven by a passion for the arts and a thirst for learning.

With a diverse background spanning over 15 years in electronic music, art, and education, I've honed an obsessive eye for detail that guides me in every new challenge I tackle. From crafting immersive room installations for exhibitions to sharing my music on international labels, I've mastered the decision-making process and held myself accountable for meticulously planned designs—right down to the smallest cable choices. Each project has instilled in me a sense of self-reliance, from securing funding to bringing unique installations and designs to life.

Now, I've found my calling in web development, where I seamlessly blend my creative stance with detail-oriented planning to craft unique web applications.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I thrive on connections and understanding, which is why I'm deeply interested in other cultures and people. Whether it's mentoring language classes in Kenya and Germany or facilitating communication mediations, I relish being in diverse environments, listening, observing, and uncovering nuances.

My journey into web development was further enriched by completing a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, where I honed my skills in UI design, backend development, and database management. I'm excited to explore opportunities where I can intersect UI with collaborative web development, particularly in aesthetic, social, or sustainable environments.
