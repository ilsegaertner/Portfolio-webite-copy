About Me

Hi, I'm Joachim, a web developer driven by the arts and fueled by a thirst for knowledge. With backgrounds in electronic music, multimedia art, education, and fine art, I integrate creativity with technical expertise. Over 15 years, my obsessive eye for detail has shaped projects, from crafting exhibitions to releasing music.

As a dedicated listener and empathic observer, I ask many questions to grasp circumstances fully and uncover subtle nuances. This skill proved invaluable in mentoring language classes and facilitating non-violent communication mediations, fostering positive social dynamics.

Completing a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp refined my expertise in UI design, backend development, and database management, driving user-centric web applications. Eager to intersect between UI and collaborative web development, I seek organizations valuing innovation in aesthetic, social, or sustainable environments.
